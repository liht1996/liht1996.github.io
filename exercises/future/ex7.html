A visualization exercise platform by VisLab

Google has developed a demo called the Rhythm of Food: http://rhythm-of-food.net/

It shows the popularity of different fruits in different seasons. Suppose we also know the statistics for different regions (the popularity of different fruits in different states/countries in different seasons), how can you improve the visualization to show the geographical information?

The data is in 5 dimensions: (fruit name, month, year, state name, search number)

Please draw your design (mapping from data to visual encoding scheme) and list a few tasks which can be better answered with your designs.
